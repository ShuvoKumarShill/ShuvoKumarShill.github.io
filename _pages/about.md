# Driving Digital Transformation Through AI & Data Analytics

Welcome! I'm **Shuvo Kumar Shill**, a technology professional specializing in artificial intelligence, data analytics, and enterprise system engineering. With over 6 years of hands-on experience in IT infrastructure management and digital transformation, I bridge the gap between cutting-edge technology and practical business solutions.

## Professional Overview

As a System Support Engineer at the **Ministry of Foreign Affairs** in Dhaka, Bangladesh, I lead mission-critical digital infrastructure initiatives that serve 300+ users across 81 unified websites and platforms. My work encompasses:

- **AI-Driven Solutions**: Implementing machine learning models for predictive maintenance and process optimization
- **Data Analytics Frameworks**: Developing comprehensive analytics pipelines for data-driven decision making
- **Infrastructure Excellence**: Managing enterprise IT systems with 99.9% uptime and 95% first-resolution rate
- **Digital Transformation**: Spearheading modernization projects that enhance operational efficiency
- **Technical Leadership**: Providing expert consultation and mentorship to cross-functional teams

## Core Competencies
